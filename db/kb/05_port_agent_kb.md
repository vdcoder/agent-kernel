# Port Agent Knowledge Base

Port Information & Shore Guide

---

# 1. Port Procedures

## Docked Ports

The ship ties directly to a pier.

Guests disembark using the ship's gangway.

---

## Tender Ports

The ship anchors offshore.

Small tender boats transport guests between the ship and shore.

---

## Going Ashore

Before leaving the ship, guests should bring:

- Cruise card (required to leave and re-board)
- Any required identification

Always verify the published **All Aboard** time.

> **Critical:** The ship will **not** wait for late guests.

Guests are responsible for returning before the All Aboard time.

---

## If You Miss the Ship

If you fail to return before departure:

1. Contact the port agent immediately.
2. Arrange transportation at your own expense to the ship's next port of call.

# 3. Cozumel, Mexico

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Currency | Mexican Peso (USD widely accepted) |
| Language | Spanish (English common in tourist areas) |
| Dock Type | Docked (Two cruise piers) |
| Distance to Town | Approximately 1 mile ($5 taxi) |

## Top Attractions

- Chankanaab Park
- San Gervasio Ruins
- Paradise Beach
- Downtown Shopping

## Local Tips

- USD is accepted almost everywhere.
- Change may be returned in pesos.
- Negotiate taxi fares before departure.
- Beach clubs typically cost **$35–65/day** (all-inclusive).

---

# 4. Grand Cayman

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Currency | Cayman Islands Dollar (CI$) (USD accepted at approximately 1 CI$ = 0.80 USD) |
| Language | English |
| Dock Type | Tender Port |
| Distance to Beach | Approximately 20-minute taxi ride to Seven Mile Beach |

## Top Attractions

- Stingray City (Must-do)
- Seven Mile Beach
- Turtle Centre
- Hell

## Local Tips

- Expect 15–30 minute waits for tender boats.
- Grand Cayman is generally an expensive destination.
- Booking Stingray City through the ship is recommended.

---

# 5. Falmouth, Jamaica

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Currency | Jamaican Dollar (JMD) (USD widely accepted) |
| Language | English |
| Dock Type | Docked (Modern port) |
| Distance to Town | Walking distance |

## Top Attractions

- Dunn's River Falls (~1.5 hours away)
- Martha Brae Rafting
- Luminous Lagoon

## Local Tips

- Street vendors may be persistent.
- Ship-sponsored excursions are recommended for Dunn's River Falls.
- Try authentic Jamaican jerk chicken.
