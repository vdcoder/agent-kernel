# Service Agent Knowledge Base

Guest Services & Requests

---

# 1. Service Request Types

| Service Type | Typical Requests | Typical Response Time |
|--------------|------------------|----------------------|
| Housekeeping | Extra towels, pillows, blankets, toiletries | 15–30 minutes |
| Housekeeping | Cabin cleaning, evening turn-down service | 20–45 minutes |
| Maintenance | Air conditioning, plumbing, television, safe, lighting | 30–60 minutes |
| Room Service | Food and beverage delivery | 30–45 minutes |
| Amenities | Iron, hair dryer, crib, bed rails | 15–30 minutes |

---

# 2. Requesting Service

Guests may request service using any of the following methods:

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Phone | Dial **0** from the stateroom to reach Guest Services |
| Stateroom TV | Select **"Request Service"** from the main menu |
| Ship App | Navigate to **Services → Make Request** |
| In Person | Visit the Guest Services Desk (Deck 9) |
| Stateroom Attendant | Speak directly or leave a written note |

---

# 3. Housekeeping Services

## Daily Cabin Service

Cabins are serviced **twice daily**:

- Morning service: **8:00 AM–12:00 PM**
- Evening turn-down: **6:00 PM–9:00 PM**

---

## Do Not Disturb

Guests may request privacy by:

- Using the door sign
- Enabling the Do Not Disturb setting through the stateroom TV

---

## Available Upon Request

Guests may request:

- Extra towels
- Washcloths
- Bath mats
- Additional pillows
- Additional blankets
- Toiletries (shampoo, soap, lotion)
- Ice bucket refills
- Additional hangers
- Laundry bags

---

# 4. Maintenance Requests

## Common Maintenance Issues

Maintenance can assist with:

- Air conditioning temperature adjustment
- Plumbing issues (toilet, shower, sink)
- Television or entertainment system problems
- Safe malfunction
- Lighting issues
- Door lock or cruise card reader problems
- Mini-bar refrigerator issues

---

## Maintenance Process

1. Report the issue to Guest Services.
2. A technician will be dispatched.
3. If cabin access is required, Guest Services will coordinate a convenient time.

---

## Urgent Maintenance

Immediately contact Guest Services for:

- Water leaks
- Electrical hazards
- Any safety concern
