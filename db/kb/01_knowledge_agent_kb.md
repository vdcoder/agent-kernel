# 3. Ship Policies

## 3.1 Dress Code

### Casual
- Comfortable daytime attire.
- Shorts and t-shirts acceptable.
- Swimwear must be covered outside pool areas.

### Smart Casual
- Main Dining Room dinner attire.
- Collared shirts.
- Dress pants or skirts.
- No shorts or flip-flops.

### Formal Night
- Evening gowns
- Cocktail dresses
- Suits
- Tuxedos (Main Dining Room)

### Not Permitted
- Swimwear
- Bare feet
- Tank tops (men)
- Cut-off shorts in dining venues

---

## 3.2 Smoking Policy

Smoking is prohibited in:

- All indoor areas
- Staterooms
- Balconies

Designated smoking areas:

- Deck 16 — Starboard Side (Outdoor)
- Deck 12 — Casino designated section

Violation fee:

- $250 cleaning fee for smoking outside designated areas.

---

## 3.3 Alcohol Policy

### Minimum Age

- 21 years
- 18 years in Europe/South America with parental consent

### Bringing Alcohol Onboard

- One bottle of wine or champagne per stateroom.
- $15 corkage fee.

# 4. Safety Information

## Muster Drill

- Mandatory before departure.
- Watch the safety video.
- Proceed to your assigned muster station.

## Muster Station

- Listed on cruise card.
- Listed on stateroom door.

## Life Jackets

- Located in the upper shelf of the stateroom closet.

## Emergency Signal

Seven short blasts followed by one long blast.

Guests should immediately proceed to their muster station.

## Medical Center

Location:

- Deck 3

Availability:

- Staffed 24/7

Regular Hours:

- 8:00 AM–12:00 PM
- 4:00 PM–8:00 PM

Emergency:

- Dial 911

# 5. Frequently Asked Questions

## What time can I board?

Boarding begins at **11:00 AM**.

Suite and Platinum+ guests may board at **10:30 AM**.

---

## What time must I return from port?

All Aboard is **30 minutes before departure**.

Refer to the daily newsletter.

---

## Is WiFi available?

| Package | Price |
|---------|------:|
| Basic | $15/day |
| Streaming | $25/day |
| Premium | $35/day |

---

## Can I bring food from ports?

- Fresh produce and meat are prohibited.
- Packaged snacks are permitted.

---

## Food allergy accommodations?

Inform the maître d' and your server.

Chefs can accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice.

---

## Is room service available?

- Continental breakfast: Free
- Other orders: $7.95
- Suites: Free

---

## Laundry options?

Self-service laundry:

- Deck 6
- Deck 8
- Deck 10

Pricing:

- Wash: $3
- Dry: $3

Full-service laundry is also available.

---

## What currency is used onboard?

US Dollars.

All purchases are charged to the onboard account and settled at the end of the cruise.
