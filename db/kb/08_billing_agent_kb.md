# Billing Agent Knowledge Base

Onboard Account & Billing Guide

---

# 1. Onboard Account Overview

The cruise card functions as the guest's onboard charge card.

All onboard purchases are charged to the guest's folio and settled at the end of the cruise.

## Account Setup

Guests may activate their onboard account by:

- Registering a credit or debit card
- Depositing cash at Guest Services

## Currency

All onboard charges are processed in **U.S. Dollars (USD).**

## Viewing Your Account

Guests may review their account through:

- Stateroom TV
- Ship App
- Guest Services

---

# 4. Automatic Charges

## Daily Gratuities

Automatically charged:

- Standard guests: **$18.00/person/day**
- Suite guests: **$20.50/person/day**

These gratuities are distributed among ship staff.

---

## Service Charges

An automatic **18% service charge** is added to:

- Bar purchases
- Spa services
- Specialty dining
- Room service

---

## Gratuity Adjustments

Guests may request gratuity adjustments through **Guest Services**.

---

# 5. Managing Your Account

## Viewing Charges

Accounts may be viewed through:

- Stateroom TV ("My Account")
- Ship App
- Guest Services

---

## Final Statement

Delivered to the guest's stateroom on the final evening of the cruise.

---

## Disputing Charges

If a guest believes a charge is incorrect:

1. Visit Guest Services with the cruise card.
2. Provide:
   - Date
   - Amount
   - Description of the issue
3. Keep all receipts.
4. Most billing disputes are resolved within **24 hours**.

---

## Post-Cruise Billing Questions

Guests should contact **Guest Relations** within **30 days** regarding onboard billing questions.

---

# 6. Account Settlement

## Credit Card Accounts

- Automatically settled on the final night.
- Receipt delivered to the stateroom.
- Receipt also sent by email.

---

## Cash Accounts

Guests should visit **Guest Services** on the final morning to settle any remaining balance.

---

## Express Checkout

Guests with a registered credit card are automatically enrolled in Express Checkout.

---

## Post-Cruise Billing Disputes

Billing disputes submitted after the cruise should be filed within **60 days** and include supporting documentation.
