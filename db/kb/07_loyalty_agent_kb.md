# Loyalty Agent Knowledge Base

Ocean Rewards Loyalty Program

---

# 1. Program Overview

Ocean Rewards automatically enrolls guests on their first cruise.

The program rewards loyalty through points, tier benefits, and redeemable rewards.

## Points Validity

Points expire **3 years** after the guest's last cruise activity.

---

# 2. Loyalty Tiers

| Tier | Points Required | Approximate Cruises |
|------|----------------:|--------------------|
| Blue | 0 | Starting Tier |
| Silver | 10,000 | 1–2 Cruises |
| Gold | 25,000 | 3–4 Cruises |
| Platinum | 50,000 | 7–8 Cruises |
| Diamond | 100,000 | 14+ Cruises |
| Diamond Plus | 200,000 | 28+ Cruises |

---

# 3. Earning Points

## Standard Earnings

| Activity | Points Earned |
|----------|--------------:|
| Cruise Night | 1,000 per night |
| Suite Night | 2,000 per night |
| Onboard Spending | 1 point per $1 spent |
| Shore Excursions | 1 point per $1 spent |
| Future Cruise Deposit | 500 bonus points |

## Bonus Opportunities

| Bonus | Points |
|-------|-------:|
| Double Points Cruise | Promotional |
| First Cruise | 2,500 |
| Referral | 5,000 |
| Birthday Bonus | 1,000 |

---

# 4. Tier Benefits

## Silver

Benefits include:

- Priority check-in
- Welcome gift
- 10% Spa discount
- Member events

---

## Gold

Includes all Silver benefits, plus:

- One complimentary specialty dinner
- Priority tender boarding
- 20% Spa discount
- Complimentary laundry bag
- 10% onboard shopping discount

---

## Platinum

Includes all Gold benefits, plus:

- Suite Lounge access (4 visits)
- Complimentary Basic WiFi
- Two complimentary specialty dinners
- 30% Spa discount
- Dedicated member phone line

---

## Diamond

Includes all Platinum benefits, plus:

- Unlimited Suite Lounge access
- Complimentary Premium WiFi
- Unlimited specialty dining
- 50% Spa discount
- Personal concierge
- Guaranteed cabin upgrade

---

| Reward | Points Required | Value |
|--------|----------------:|------|

| WiFi (7 Days) | 4,000 | $105 |

## Redemption Methods

Guests may redeem points through:

- Online account
- Guest Services
- Member phone line

## Redemption Rules

- Points are non-transferable.
