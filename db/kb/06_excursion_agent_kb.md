# 1. Shore Excursion Information

Guests may book shore excursions through:

- Shore Excursions Desk (Deck 9)

---

## Cancellation Policy

| Cancellation Time | Refund |
|-------------------|--------|
| More than 48 hours | Full refund |
| 24–48 hours | 50% refund |
| Less than 24 hours | No refund |

---

## Meeting Point

**Shore Excursions Lounge (Deck 9)**

---

## Benefits of Booking Through the Ship

- Ship waits if a ship-sponsored excursion returns late.
- Transportation to the next port is provided if a ship-sponsored excursion causes guests to miss departure.
- Vetted excursion operators.
- English-speaking guides.

# 5. Featured Excursions

## GC01 — Stingray City Sandbar

### Description

Boat ride to the famous Stingray City sandbar where guests stand in waist-deep water with friendly Southern Atlantic stingrays.

Guests can:

- Hold stingrays
- Feed stingrays
- Take photographs

### Includes

- Boat transportation
- Stingray interaction
- Life jackets
- Squid for feeding

### Bring

- Swimsuit
- Towel
- Waterproof camera
- Reef-safe sunscreen

### Requirements

- Ability to climb a boat ladder.
- Ability to stand in shallow water.
- Life jackets are available.

### Notes

- Water shoes are recommended.
- Southern Atlantic stingrays used on the excursion are safe and do not have dangerous stinging barbs.

---

## JM01 — Dunn's River Falls Climb

### Description

Climb the famous 600-foot cascading waterfall in a guided human chain led by expert guides.

### Includes

- Round-trip transportation (approximately 1.5 hours each way)
- Entrance fee
- Guide
- Locker

### Bring

- Water shoes (required)
- Swimsuit
- Change of clothes
- Waterproof camera

### Requirements

- Strenuous activity.
- Wet, slippery rocks.
- Not recommended for guests with:
  - Mobility issues
  - Heart conditions
  - Pregnancy

### Notes

- Non-climbers may observe from viewing decks.
- Beach access is available at the base of the falls.
- Minimum age: 4 years (with parent).
