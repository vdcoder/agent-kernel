# Booking Agent Knowledge Base

Reservation Management Guide

---

# 1. Reservation Types

| Reservation Type | Booking Methods | Advance Booking |
|------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Main Dining (Traditional) | Assigned during cruise booking | Pre-assigned |
| Main Dining (Flexible) | Walk-in or hostess | Same day |
| Specialty Restaurants | Ship App, Restaurant, Guest Services | Recommended |
| Spa Treatments | Ship App, Spa Reception | Highly Recommended |
| Reserved Shows | Ship App, Theater Box Office | Recommended |
| Shore Excursions | Ship App, Shore Excursions Desk | Recommended |

---

# 2. Booking Methods

Guests may make reservations using any of the following methods.

## Ship App

The preferred booking method.

Guests can:

- Browse availability
- Make reservations
- Modify reservations
- Cancel reservations

---

## Stateroom TV

Navigate to:

**Reservations**

from the main menu.

---

## In Person

| Reservation | Location |
|-------------|----------|
| Restaurants | Hostess Stand |
| Spa | Spa Reception (Deck 13) |
| Shows | Theater Box Office (Deck 11) |
| Shore Excursions | Shore Excursions Desk (Deck 9) |

---

## Phone

Guests may call the extension for the desired venue.

---

# 3. Reservation Changes

## Dining Reservations

### Modification

- Up to **2 hours** before the reservation.

### Cancellation

- Up to **2 hours** before the reservation.
- No cancellation penalty.

### No-Show Policy

Missing a reservation may affect future booking priority.

---

## Spa Appointments

### Modification

24-hour notice is preferred.

### Cancellation

| Notice | Charge |
|--------|--------|
| 24 hours or more | Full refund |
| Less than 24 hours | 50% charge |

---

## Shore Excursions

| Cancellation Time | Refund |
|-------------------|--------|
| More than 48 hours before port arrival | Full refund |
| 24–48 hours before port arrival | 50% refund |
| Less than 24 hours before port arrival | No refund |

---

# 4. Availability & Booking Tips

## High Demand Periods

### Specialty Dining

Most popular between:

**7:00 PM–8:00 PM**

---

### Spa

- Sea Days: Highest demand
- Port Days: Better availability

---

### Reserved Shows

Most popular:

- Opening Night
- Final Night

---

## Tips for Getting Reservations

- Book before the cruise whenever possible.
- Check the Ship App on the first morning onboard.
- Ask to join cancellation waitlists.
- Consider early or late dining times.
- Loyalty members receive priority access.

---

# 5. Reservation Confirmation

## Confirmation Number

Every reservation includes a confirmation number.

Guests are encouraged to save it.

---

## Reminders

Reservation reminders are sent to:

- Ship App
- Stateroom TV

---

## Day of Reservation

Guests should:

- Check the Daily Newsletter for schedule changes.
- Bring their Cruise Card.
- Bring the confirmation number if available (helpful but not required).
- Arrive **5–10 minutes early**.

---

# 6. Party Size Guidelines

| Venue | Maximum Party Size | Large Group Option |
|--------|-------------------:|--------------------|
| Specialty Restaurants | 8 | Private dining available |
| Spa (Couples) | 2 | Group packages available |
| Reserved Shows | 10 | Request adjacent seating |
| Shore Excursions | Varies | Private tours available |
