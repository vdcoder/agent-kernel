# Tender Confusion in Grand Cayman

Guest asked whether they could return to the ship any time during Grand Cayman.
Assistant explained that Grand Cayman is a tender port, tenders run throughout the day, and guests should expect 15–30 minute waits.

Guest was concerned about missing All Aboard at 3:30 PM.
Assistant advised returning early and treating 3:00 PM as a safer personal deadline.
