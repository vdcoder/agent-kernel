# Embarkation Day WiFi Advice

Guest wanted to purchase WiFi immediately after boarding but was unsure whether to buy before or after embarkation.

Assistant suggested waiting until boarding was complete, connecting through the ship's network, and comparing available packages before purchasing.

Guest was also informed that many travelers initially purchase Premium WiFi but later downgrade after evaluating actual usage.

Guest selected the Streaming package after reviewing expected usage and expressed satisfaction with the recommendation.
