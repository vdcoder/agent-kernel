# Spa Booking Strategy on Sea Days

Guest wanted a relaxing spa appointment during a sea day but found most treatments fully booked.

Assistant explained that sea days are consistently the busiest spa days and suggested booking before embarkation, choosing a morning appointment, and considering a port-day treatment for greater availability.

Guest booked a massage on a port day and reported a quieter, more relaxed experience.
