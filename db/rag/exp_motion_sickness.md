# Motion Sickness Prevention

First-time cruiser expressed concern about seasickness before departure.

Assistant explained that motion varies with weather and itinerary but suggested practical steps: pack motion sickness medication, choose a mid-ship cabin when possible, spend time on open decks if symptoms appear, and stay hydrated.

Guest later reported only mild discomfort and appreciated receiving the advice before symptoms developed.
