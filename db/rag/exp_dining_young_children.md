# Dining Time Recommendation for Family with Young Children

Guest traveling with two children (ages 4 and 7) requested advice on choosing between traditional dining and flexible dining.

Assistant explained the differences between the dining options and suggested selecting the earliest available dinner seating.

Families with younger children often have a more enjoyable experience with earlier dinner times because children are less tired, specialty restaurants are less crowded, and evening entertainment remains available afterward.

Guest selected early dining and later expressed appreciation for the recommendation.
